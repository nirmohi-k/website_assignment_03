Mapping Public Spaces & Transit in NYC

This map examines the distribution of open space parks, privately owned public spaces (POPS), pedestrian plazas under the NYC Plaza Program, and ADA-accessible subway stations across New York City. By visualizing these elements together, the map highlights the relationship between public space, transit accessibility, and private contributions to the urban fabric, offering insights into connectivity, spatial distribution, and gaps in access to open spaces.
